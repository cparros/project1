    THE RULES OF BLACKJACK
  1. The goal for blackjack is beating the sum of the dealer’s hand without going over a total of 21.
  2. All numbered cards are worth their number
  3. All Face Cards (Jack, Queen, King) are worth 10.
  4. Aces are worth 1 or 11. Whichever will make a better hand.
  5. Each player starts with two cards
  6. The dealer also begins with two cards however one of the dealer’s cards is hidden until the end of the game.
  7. To “hit” is to ask for another card. To “stand” is to hold on to your total and end your turn.
  8. If the sum of the players cards is over 21 means that you bust, and the dealer wins.
  9. If you tie with the dealer, it is counted as a push and you both get your money back.
  10. If you are dealt 21 at the start (Ace & 10), you have a Blackjack.
  11. The dealer will hit until their cards total 17 or higher.
  12. Doubling is like a hit, only the bet is doubled and you only get one more card(can’t hit again after doubling and can’t double on the second turn.).
